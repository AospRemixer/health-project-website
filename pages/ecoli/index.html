What is a Injury

An injury or illness is an abnormal condition or disorder. Injuries include cases such as, but not limited to, a cut, fracture, sprain, or amputation. Illnesses include both acute and chronic illnesses, such as, but not limited to, a skin disease, respiratory disorder, or poisoning. Basically an injury is a what causes a temporary or permanent damage to the body!

Types Of Injuries

Spinal Cord Injury: Spinal cord injures are caused by damage to the spinal cord, usually permanent. When the Spinal cord get damage it can cause paralysis. This means that a body part can freeze because the spinal cord cant correctly send signals to the body part.
Broken Bones: Broken bone injuries occur when something harder then the bone itself strucks the bone. This causes the bone to break, usually broken bones can heal but sometimes the damage is too strong and causes the bone to break permanently. Usually when something like this happens the docters will do a surgery to put it back together. When you have a broken bone, you will face pain, and fear of breaking the bone.
Concussion: Concussions are usually mistaken by broken bones, but thats not true. Concussion is when your skull gets damaged, usually by a impact against something stronger then the skull. This causes the skull to be damaged. The brain moves back and forth rapidly because of the damage. Concussions are serious and if you ever think you have one then report it to a docter immediately.
Laceration: Laceration is in simple words, a cut. You can get a laceration from something sharp, something that can cut through your skin layer. A laceration occurs when something sharp cuts through your skin layer. When it goes further then the skin layer, like all the way to the muscle layer. Then docters recomend getting medical help. That type of deep laceration can get dangerous. Besides that, most laceration are only through the skin layer. That type of laceration can be healed by itself, and doesnt require much suppourt.
Brain Injury: Brain injury is when a injury causes the brain to behave different then most other brains. Sometimes people have a brain of a child, but age of a adult. The adult person might behave like a child because his brain isn't behaving properly. Brain injury causes the neuronal activity in the brain to behave different. All this can be caused by one single injury.
Death: Death is a irreversible cessation. There is no typical way to FIX or UNDO death. Its the biological ending of a living species. This is when the dead body starts to decompose. Death can occur by many things. Fortunately death is pretty rare, you wont see many people dying everyday.
