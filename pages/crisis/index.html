What is a Service?

We all don't want someone close to us to die, it is very sad and can ruin many things. Someone can ruin there whole life by getting depressed about something, then they try to suciding. No one will ever want such a thing to occur, but it happens. Thats why many people have come up with services that keep people away from doing such things. For many years these services have been saving many peoples lives and are still doing so. There are diferent services for diferent things, some for sucide control while others are for injury protection.

Sucide Control

Asking for help can be hard. That’s why they offer a safe place to talk - any time, in your own way. If you are having thoughts of suicide, you don’t have to face it alone. They are available if you need a safe and judgement free place to talk. Their responders are here to listen to you, support you, and keep you safe.

Sucide Control Website

Poison Control

OPC [Ontario Poison Center] are available to help you and your family and to answer questions about poisons 24 hours a day, every day of the year. They can help people of all ages in more than 170 languages. If you think someone has been poisoned get help now.

Poison Control Website

BullyingCanada

BullyingCanada has stood up against cyberbullying, they protect many teens/kids from cyberbullying. We all know cyberbullying can be very depressing and horrible. So BullyingCanada has stood up with many kids/teens around the world to stop cyberbullying. Their amazing service is just a call away. Know if you ever get cyberbullied, you will know where to call.
